About Me

I’m a software engineer based in Manchester. My primary focus is on backend development, but I’m also skilled in frontend technologies. While C# is my main language, I also have experience in JavaScript, TypeScript, and Python, making me a versatile developer across the stack. I specialize in building scalable, reliable systems with strong expertise in infrastructure, DevOps practices, and Infrastructure as Code (IaC) tools such as Docker, Kubernetes, and Terraform. I have extensive knowledge of cloud platforms like Azure, AWS, and Cloudflare, as well as server management in both Linux and Windows Server environments. I design and automate deployment pipelines, implement efficient CI/CD workflows, and drive end-to-end system automation to enhance operational efficiency and consistency.

My background also includes extensive experience in Tier 3 support desk engineering and modern workplace management (MDM) with Microsoft Intune. Additionally, I hold certifications in various technologies, demonstrating my commitment to staying current with industry standards.

When I’m not at work, I sometimes channel my creativity into graphic design, where I bring my artistic ideas to life through visually engaging projects. This passion allows me to explore new design trends and refine my creative thinking, often inspiring fresh perspectives in my engineering work. I also enjoy music production and DJing, and like experimenting with sound to craft unique experiences. Gaming is another big part of my life. These hobbies help me stay inspired and balanced, contributing to my personal and professional growth.
